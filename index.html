WI4 WebGL Demo

Rotating 3D letters & numbers with animated colors and outlines.

Controls

  • Space Pause / resume animation
  • ▲ Increase rotation speed
  • ▼ Decrease rotation speed
  • Click Toggle freeze

About

This canvas renders custom 3D geometry for the characters W, I, and 4 using raw WebGL. Vertices, colors, and indices are defined manually in data.js, then animated and shaded in main.js.
